# Quick Research: Per-Vendor Unit Orders Under Replicator

## Question
What are the specific per-vendor unit orders under the U.S. DoD Replicator initiative?

## Findings

Exact per-vendor unit order quantities remain partially classified, but key data points are:

### MAPC (Maritime Applied Physics Corporation) - GARC
- Production ramped to **32 units per month** (384/year capacity) as of 2025
- Under a **$160M commitment** from the US DoD
- 16-foot autonomous craft at less than $1M per unit
- "Hundreds delivered" to USV Squadrons 3 and 7
- Widely assessed as the primary Hellscape/Replicator small USV program

### Saronic - Corsair
- Secured a **$392M U.S. Navy contract**
- Manufactures three USV models ranging from 6 to 24 feet
- Has not disclosed specific production rates
- Planning "Port Alpha" manufacturing facility to scale production to MAPC-like rates

### Overall Replicator Funding
- The DoD set aside approximately **$1 billion in funding** for FY24 and FY25 Replicator work
- Program focused on swarming unmanned systems (air, surface, undersea)
- As of September 2025, DoD touted "successful transition" but questions about delivery timelines lingered
- A CRS report to Congress in August 2025 addressed ongoing oversight questions

### Other Vendors
- Specific allocations for other participants in the Replicator USV tranche have not been publicly disaggregated
